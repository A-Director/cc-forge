## Launch readiness gate

A project is ready for public launch when:
- Domains 01-08 are at 100% (done or not-applicable with decision record)
- No CRITICAL or HIGH items in RISKS.md without mitigation
- No BLOCK outcomes on any gate review

Domains 09-10 (Growth, Operations) are post-launch — they do not block launch.

## Status key

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `not-started` | Not yet worked on |
| `in-progress` | Being worked on this sprint |
| `done` | Complete — evidence recorded |
| `not-applicable` | Does not apply to this project — decision in DECISIONS.md |

---

## Override rule

Any item marked `not-applicable` MUST have:
1. An entry in `DECISIONS.md` explaining why
2. An entry in `RISKS.md` if the item relates to security, reliability, or compliance

Hermes enforces this — a `not-applicable` without a decision record is flagged
by Argus as non-compliant.
